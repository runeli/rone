OEE explained.

Availability

represents the percentage of scheduled time that the operation is available to operate. Often referred to as Uptime.

Performance

represents the speed at which the Work Center runs as a percentage of its designed speed.

Recovery

represents the percentage of raw material utilization.

Quality

represents the Good Units produced as a percentage of the Total Units Started. Commonly referred to as First Pass Yield
